About Me

I am a graduate student at Northwestern’s Medill School of Journalism. Prior to my time at Northwestern, I attended the University of California, San Diego, where I received Bachelor’s Degrees in both Psychology and Communications. While I was there, I was a member of the swim team, and wrote for the sports section of the school newspaper. My love for sports has followed me throughout my life, and has contributed to me pursuing sports media at Medill.

Outside of Medill, I do work as a contract writer for the magazine SwimSwam, and as a data journalist for Sportradar. When I’m not working, I enjoy watching TV and movies and spending time with friends and family. While I currently live in Chicago, I am originally from the San Francisco Bay Area.
